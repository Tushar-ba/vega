About Us

M/s Vega Enterprises Bangalore is a trusted and well established company since 2006 , we are a professional and result-oriented team of engineers and skilled tech to optimize your workspace performance and ensure success with superior facilities management (FM) and maintenance services.
Our goal is to continually exceed client expectations. We Strive to partner in clients growth by addressing their future plan and assisting in expansion as a strategic partner. We provide support our clients operations in all engineering services and make certain that our clients have a healthy and safe environment. We provide comprehensive support services required at client's workspace. This helps clients to focus on their core bussiness needs, confident in the fact that all support services are functioning at maximum efficiency. We are undertaking all kinds of engineering services jobs to a wide range of hospitallity industries, such as Hotels, Hospitals, Shopping Malls, Pharmaceutical companies, Automobile and Textile industiries among others.

What we Do

We are undertaking Trunkey Projects in Engineering including construction, MEP / HVAC/ Fire system, supplying of various Industrial Equipments which applies to engineering services, supplying of chemicals for the water treatment process, used in various engineering applications in heavy Industries, and all kind of facilities management services, These applications include Air-conditioning systems, Cooling Towers, RO Plants. Heat Exchangers, STP/ETP, and more... Vega Enterprises, ability to handle projects of any size or complexity. Whether it's a small-scale or a large-scale project, we have the expertise to coordinate different contractors and vendors effectively, ensuring a cost- effective and timely completion of projects, we offer a wide range of services, including new construction, remodelling, additions, preventative maintenance, and service upgrades. Our commitment to customer satisfaction is unwavering, and we always strive to help our clients achieve their goals. Our team consists of highly skilled professionals with experience in a diverse range of projects, from simple power connections to complex full factory automation. We are constantly on the lookout for innovative solutions and will go the extra mile to deliver the best service you've ever experienced. Rest assured, if there's a better way to get the job done. we will find it and implement it for you.
